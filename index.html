Brief Biography

         I'm currently a postdoctoral researcher in the DS3Lab, Systems group, ETH Zürich, working with Prof. Ce Zhang.
        My research interests focus on big data and database systems. Currently, I'm working on in-database machine learning, distributed stream processing, distributed machine learning, and memory management techniques. I received my PhD Degree from Institute of Software, Chinese Academy of Sciences and my Bachelor's Degree from Wuhan University.
